# Bloodlust
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 9*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Powerful Recovery.** When you roll a [Hit Point Die](/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) to regain [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), you can treat any roll of 1 or 2 as a 3.

**Sanguine Feast.** Once per turn when you hit a [Bloodied](/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied) creature that isn't a Construct or Undead with an attack roll, you can expend a [Hit Point Die](/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md), roll it, and regain a number of [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the number rolled plus your Constitution modifier.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
