# Street Justice
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 41*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Headlock.** Your allies have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls against a creature [Grappled](/rules/conditions.md#Grappled) by you.

**Sturdy Knot.** When you use [Chain](/items/chain-xphb.md), [Manacles](/items/manacles-xphb.md), or [Rope](/items/rope-xphb.md) to bind a creature, add your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md) to the DC to escape or burst the [Chain](/items/chain-xphb.md), [Manacles](/items/manacles-xphb.md), or [Rope](/items/rope-xphb.md).

**Tough Talk.** A creature's [Hostile](/rules/variant-rules/hostile-attitude-xphb.md) attitude doesn't impose [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on your Charisma ([Intimidation](/rules/skills.md#Intimidation)) checks to influence that creature.
