# Greater Mark of Passage
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 44*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Intuitive Motion benefit of your [Mark of Passage](/feats/mark-of-passage-efa.md) feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

**Improved Passage.** When you use the Magical Passage benefit of your [Mark of Passage](/feats/mark-of-passage-efa.md) feat to cast [Misty Step](/spells/misty-step-xphb.md) without a spell slot, you can also choose up to two willing creatures you can see within 30 feet of yourself before you teleport. Each target can then take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to also teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space it can see.
