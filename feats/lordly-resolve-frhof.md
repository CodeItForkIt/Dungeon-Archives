# Lordly Resolve
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 40*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Standard Bearer.** As a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), choose up to three creatures within 60 feet of yourself that can see you. Each target can immediately take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to right itself and end the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition, provided its [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) isn't 0. Additionally, you bolster the targets' resolve, which lasts for 1 minute or until you have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition. While bolstered, a target can't be possessed or gain the [Charmed](/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) or [Frightened](/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition; if a target is already possessed, [Charmed](/rules/conditions.md#Charmed), or [Frightened](/rules/conditions.md#Frightened), the target has [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on any new saving throw against the relevant effect.

Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
