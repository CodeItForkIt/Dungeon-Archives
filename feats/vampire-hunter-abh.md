# Vampire Hunter
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 8*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Adroit Escape.** You have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on checks to escape from nonmagical restraints or the [Grappled](/rules/conditions.md#Grappled) condition.

**Vitality Ward.** When you take Necrotic damage, you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to mitigate the damage. Roll a number of d6s equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and add them together. Reduce the Necrotic damage you take by this total. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
