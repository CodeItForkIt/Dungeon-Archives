# Spellfire Spark
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 38*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Magic Absorption.** Once per turn, when you take damage from a spell or magical effect, you reduce the total damage taken by `1d4`. You can't use this benefit if you have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.

**Spellfire Flame.** You learn the [Sacred Flame](/spells/sacred-flame-xphb.md) cantrip. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat). You can also cast this cantrip as a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
