# Purple Dragon Rook
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 38*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Entreat.** You gain proficiency in one of the following skills: [Insight](/rules/skills.md#Insight), [Performance](/rules/skills.md#Performance), or [Persuasion](/rules/skills.md#Persuasion).

**Rallying Cry.** When you roll [Initiative](/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) and don't have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition, you can choose a number of creatures equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md) that you can see within 30 feet of yourself. Those creatures gain [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md).

Once you use this benefit, you can't do so again until you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
