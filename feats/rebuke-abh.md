# Rebuke
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 10*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Radiant Strike.** When you make a damage roll that deals Radiant damage, you can cause one Huge or smaller creature taking the damage to have the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
