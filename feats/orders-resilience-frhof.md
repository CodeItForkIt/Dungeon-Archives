# Order's Resilience
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 40*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Resurge.** When you have the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition, you can right yourself with only 5 feet of movement.

**Stronger Together.** If you are within 5 feet of an ally that doesn't have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition, you and that ally have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Strength saving throws. You can't use this benefit while you have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.
