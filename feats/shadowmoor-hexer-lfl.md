# Shadowmoor Hexer
*Source: Lorwyn: First Light*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Hex.** You always have the [Hex](/spells/hex-xphb.md) spell prepared. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat). You can cast it once without a spell slot, and you regain the ability to cast it in that way when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have.

**Curse Magic.** When a creature that you've cursed with [Hex](/spells/hex-xphb.md) hits you with an attack roll, the creature takes Psychic damage equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md). A creature takes this damage only once per turn.
