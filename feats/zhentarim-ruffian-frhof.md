# Zhentarim Ruffian
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 38*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Exploit Opening.** When you roll damage for an [Opportunity Attack](/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack), you can roll the damage dice twice and use either roll against the target.

**Family First.** If you have [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md) when you roll [Initiative](/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md), you can expend it to give yourself and your allies [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on that [Initiative](/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md) roll.
