# Purple Dragon Commandant
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 40*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th or Proficiency with martial weapons

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Encourage Ally.** As a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you bolster one ally you can see within 30 feet. The ally gains [Temporary Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) equal to `2d6` plus the modifier of the ability score increased by this feat. You can take this [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Last Stand.** You have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on attack rolls while [Bloodied](/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied).
