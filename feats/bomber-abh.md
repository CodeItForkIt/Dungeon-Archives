# Bomber
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 9*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Dexterity score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Far Lobber.** When you use the [Attack](/rules/actions.md#Attack) action to throw a vial or flask, you can target an object or creature you can see within 40 feet of yourself.

**Long Shots.** Attacking at long range doesn't impose [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on your attack rolls with [Thrown](/rules/item-properties.md#Thrown) weapons.
