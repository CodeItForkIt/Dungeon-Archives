# Greater Mark of Sentinel
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 44*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Sentinel's Intuition benefit of your [Mark of Sentinel](/feats/mark-of-sentinel-efa.md) feat, you can roll `1d6` instead of `1d4`.

**Improved Sentinel.** When you use the Vigilant Guardian benefit of your [Mark of Sentinel](/feats/mark-of-sentinel-efa.md) feat, you can also make one attack with a weapon or an [Unarmed Strike](/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md) as part of that same [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md).
