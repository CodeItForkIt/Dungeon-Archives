# Boon of the Bright Sun
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 43*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Daylight Presence.** As a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you radiate a 30-foot [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) of [Bright Light](/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) that is sunlight. If any of the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md)'s area overlaps with an area of [Darkness](/spells/darkness-xphb.md) created by a spell, that spell is dispelled. The [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) lasts until you dismiss it (no action required), die, or have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.

**Fortifying Light.** When your Daylight Presence is active, at the start of each of your turns, you and allies you can see in your Daylight Presence gain 10 [Temporary Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).
