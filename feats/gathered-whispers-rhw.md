# Gathered Whispers
*Source: RHW*  

**Prerequisite**: Ravenloft Campaign

You are haunted by a cacophony of whispering spirits only you can hear. You gain the following features.

**Spirit Whispers.** You learn the [Message](/spells/message-xphb.md) spell and can cast it without Material components. Additionally, you always have the [Augury](/spells/augury-xphb.md) spell prepared. You can cast it without a spell slot or spell components, and you must finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) before you can cast it in this way again. You can also cast the spell using any spell slots you have.

Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this benefit (choose when you select this feat).

**Unearthly Scream.** When you are hit by an attack roll, you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to channel your haunting spirits into a protective, otherworldly scream. You can add your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md) to your AC against that attack, potentially causing it to miss. You can use this benefit a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Voices from Beyond.** Immediately after you make a [D20 Test](/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) and roll a 1 on the `d20`, the haunting voices rise to a ghastly volume. Make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 13 plus your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)). On a failed save, you have the [Deafened](/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition until the end of your next turn. While [Deafened](/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), you have [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on ability checks and attack rolls.
