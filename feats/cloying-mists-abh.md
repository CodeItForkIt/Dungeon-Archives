# Cloying Mists
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 9*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Arise, Fog.** You always have the [Fog Cloud](/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md) spell prepared. You can cast it without a spell slot, and you must finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) before you can cast it in this way again. You can also cast it using spell slots you have of the appropriate level. Your spellcasting ability for the spell is the ability increased by this feat.

**Grasping Mist.** Whenever you cast [Fog Cloud](/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md), nonmagical flames in the spell's [Sphere](/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) are extinguished, and creatures other than you and your allies have their [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) reduced by 5 feet while in the spell's [Sphere](/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md).
