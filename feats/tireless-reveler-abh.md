# Tireless Reveler
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 8*  

When an ally you can see within 60 feet of yourself expends [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md), you can gain [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md) if you lack it. You can use this benefit a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Short](/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
