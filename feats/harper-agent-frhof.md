# Harper Agent
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 37*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Thieves' Cant.** You know Thieves' Cant.

**Instrument Training.** You gain proficiency with a [Musical Instrument](/items/musical-instrument-xphb.md) of your choice.

**Distracting Melody.** When you take the [Help](/rules/actions.md#Help) action to assist an ally's attack roll, the enemy you're distracting can be within 30 feet of you, rather than within 5 feet of you, provided the enemy can see or hear you.
