# Watchers
*Source: RHW*  

**Prerequisite**: Ravenloft Campaign

Something unnatural is always watching you, taking the form of scurrying vermin and other eerie creatures. You gain the following features.

**Borrowed Eyes.** You always have the [Beast Sense](/spells/beast-sense-xphb.md) and [Speak with Animals](/spells/speak-with-animals-xphb.md) spells prepared. You can cast each spell without a spell slot, and you must finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) before you can cast it in this way again. You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

**Heightened Suspicion.** Whenever you take the [Search](/rules/actions.md#Search) action, you can roll `1d4` and add the number rolled to any ability check made as part of that action.

**Incessant Watchers.** You have [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on saving throws made against the [Scrying](/spells/scrying-xphb.md) spell.

In addition, immediately after you make a [D20 Test](/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) and roll a 1 on the `d20`, paranoia threatens to overwhelm you. Make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 13 plus your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)). On a failed save, you have [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on [D20 Tests](/rules/variant-rules/d20-test-xphb.md) for 1 minute. You can repeat the save at the end of each of your turns, ending the effect early on a success.
