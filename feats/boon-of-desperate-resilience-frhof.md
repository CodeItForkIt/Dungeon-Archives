# Boon of Desperate Resilience
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 42*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Constitution by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Defense of Body and Mind.** While you are [Bloodied](/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied), you have [Resistance](/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to every damage type except Force.
