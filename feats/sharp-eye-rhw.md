# Sharp Eye
*Source: RHW*  

When you take the Search or Study action, you can give yourself [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on any ability check made as part of that action. You can use this feature a number of times equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md), and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md). If the check fails, the use of this feature isn't expended.
