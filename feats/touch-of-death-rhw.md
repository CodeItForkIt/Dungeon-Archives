# Touch of Death
*Source: RHW*  

**Prerequisite**: Ravenloft Campaign

Deathly power resides within you, bursting out at the slightest provocation. You gain the following features.

**Death Touch.** You learn the [Chill Touch](/spells/chill-touch-xphb.md) spell and can cast it without spell components. Necrotic damage you deal with this spell ignores [Resistance](/spells/resistance-xphb.md). Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for this spell (choose when you select this feat).

**Pull of the Grave.** You have [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) on [Death Saving Throws](/rules/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md).
