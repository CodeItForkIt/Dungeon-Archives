# Tyro of the Gauntlet
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 38*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Stand as One.** When an ally within 5 feet of you is subjected to an effect that would push or pull it, you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to prevent that ally from being pushed or pulled. To receive this benefit, the ally can't have the [Incapacitated](/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition.

**Vigilant.** When you take the [Ready](/rules/actions.md#Ready) action, the next attack roll made against you has [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md) before the start of your next turn.
