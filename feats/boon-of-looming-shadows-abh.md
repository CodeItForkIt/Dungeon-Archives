# Boon of Looming Shadows
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 11*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Shadowy Stretch.** When you take the [Attack](/rules/actions.md#Attack) action, your reach for your Melee weapon attacks increases by 10 feet until the end of your turn.

**Dancing Silhouette.** You can take the [Dodge](/rules/actions.md#Dodge) action as a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md).
