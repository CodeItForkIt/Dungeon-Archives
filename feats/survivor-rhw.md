# Survivor
*Source: RHW*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Hypervigilance.** Whenever you roll [Initiative](/rules/variant-rules/initiative-xphb.md), you can reroll the `d20` if the number rolled is 9 or lower. You must use the new roll.

**Steel Yourself.** When you fail a saving throw to avoid or end the [Charmed](/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) or [Frightened](/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition, you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to add a bonus to the roll, potentially causing it to succeed. The bonus is equal to your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md).

Once you take this [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md), you can't do so again until you finish a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
