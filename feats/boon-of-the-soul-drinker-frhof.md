# Boon of the Soul Drinker
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 43*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Grave Resistance.** You have [Resistance](/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Cold damage and Necrotic damage.

**Siphon Life.** When an enemy within 120 feet of you is reduced to 0 [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md), you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to regain 50 [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).
