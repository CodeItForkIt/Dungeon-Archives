# Boon of Terror
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 43*  

**Prerequisite**: 19th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Charisma score by 1, to a maximum of 30.

**Fearless.** You have [Immunity](/rules/variant-rules/immunity-xphb.md) to the [Frightened](/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition.

**Flee, Fools!.** When a creature with the [Frightened](/rules/conditions.md#Frightened) condition starts its turn within 60 feet of you, you can take a [Reaction](/rules/variant-rules/reaction-xphb.md) to stoke its terror, provided you can see the creature and it isn't behind [Total Cover](/rules/variant-rules/cover-xphb.md). If you do so, the creature must succeed on a Wisdom saving throw (DC 8 plus your Charisma modifier and your [Proficiency Bonus](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md)) or spend its turn moving away from you by the fastest available means. Once you use this benefit, you can't use it again until you finish a [Short](/rules/variant-rules/short-rest-xphb.md) or [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md).

**Intimidating.** You gain [Proficiency](/rules/variant-rules/proficiency-xphb.md) in the [Intimidation](/rules/skills.md#Intimidation) skill if you don't already have it. You also gain [Expertise](/rules/variant-rules/expertise-xphb.md) in [Intimidation](/rules/skills.md#Intimidation).
