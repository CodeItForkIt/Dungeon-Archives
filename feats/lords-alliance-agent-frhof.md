# Lords' Alliance Agent
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 38*  

You gain the following benefits.

**Inspiring Strike.** Once per turn when you score a [Critical Hit](/rules/variant-rules/critical-hit-xphb.md) against a creature, you can choose an ally within 30 feet of yourself who can see or hear you and who lacks [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md). That ally gains [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md).

**Reassert Honor.** When an enemy you can see deals damage to an ally of yours that is within 5 feet of you, you have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on your next attack roll against that enemy before the end of your next turn.
