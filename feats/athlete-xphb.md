# Athlete
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 202*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th; Strength 13 or higher or 4th; Dexterity 13 or higher

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Strength or Dexterity by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Climb Speed.** You gain a [Climb Speed](/rules/variant-rules/climb-speed-xphb.md) equal to your [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md).

**Hop Up.** When you have the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition, you can right yourself with only 5 feet of movement.

**Jumping.** You can make a running [Long](/rules/variant-rules/long-jump-xphb.md) or [High Jump](/rules/variant-rules/high-jump-xphb.md) after moving only 5 feet.
