# Rakdos Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [crown of madness](/spells/crown-of-madness-xphb.md), [hellish rebuke](/spells/hellish-rebuke-xphb.md), or [shatter](/spells/shatter-xphb.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 174*
