# Charm of Exorcism

You can use the Charm as a [Magic](/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to cast [Dispel Evil and Good](/spells/dispel-evil-and-good-xphb.md) or [Remove Curse](/spells/remove-curse-xphb.md) without requiring Material components. The Charm lasts for 7 days or until you use it. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the Charm's spells (choose each time you gain this Charm).
