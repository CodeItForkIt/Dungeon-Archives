# Manifest Charm of Lamannia, the Twilight Forest
*Charm*  

When you use a spell slot to cast a spell that conjures or summons a Beast, an Elemental, or a Fey (including [Conjure Animals](/spells/conjure-animals-xphb.md), [Conjure Elemental](/spells/conjure-elemental-xphb.md), [Conjure Fey](/spells/conjure-fey-xphb.md), [Conjure Minor Elementals](/spells/conjure-minor-elementals-xphb.md), [Conjure Woodland Beings](/spells/conjure-woodland-beings-xphb.md), [Find Steed](/spells/find-steed-xphb.md), [Summon Beast](/spells/summon-beast-xphb.md), [Summon Elemental](/spells/summon-elemental-xphb.md), or [Summon Fey](/spells/summon-fey-xphb.md)), you can expend the Charm (no action required) to treat the spell as if it were cast using a spell slot 1 level above the slot expended.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 50*
