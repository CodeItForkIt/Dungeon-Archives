# Charm of the Black Rose

You can expend this charm to gain one of the following effects:

- You have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on ability checks made to influence [Lord Soth's](/bestiary/npc/lord-soth-of-sithicus-rhw.md) attitude for 24 hours.  
- You cast [Dissonant Whispers](/spells/dissonant-whispers-xphb.md) (level 5 version, spell save DC 17).  

The Charm vanishes once you use either effect.

*Source: RHW*
