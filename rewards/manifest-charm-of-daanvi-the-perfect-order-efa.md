# Manifest Charm of Daanvi, the Perfect Order
*Charm*  

You can expend the Charm (no action required) to end the [Blinded](/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [Paralyzed](/rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), or [Poisoned](/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition on yourself.

*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 50*
