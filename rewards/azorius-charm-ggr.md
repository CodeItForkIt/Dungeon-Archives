# Azorius Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [hold person](/spells/hold-person-xphb.md) (3rd-level version), [command](/spells/command-xphb.md), or [counterspell](/spells/counterspell-xphb.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 173*
