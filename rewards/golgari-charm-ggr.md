# Golgari Charm
*Charm*  

When you activate this charm, you can cast the [dispel magic](/spells/dispel-magic-xphb.md), [false life](/spells/false-life-xphb.md) (3rd-level version), or [stinking cloud](/spells/stinking-cloud-xphb.md) spell. The charm vanishes after you activate it.

*Source: Guildmasters' Guide to Ravnica p. 173*
