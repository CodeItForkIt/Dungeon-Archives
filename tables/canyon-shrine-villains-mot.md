# Canyon Shrine Villains
*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 154* 

**Canyon Shrine Villains**

| dice: d8 | Villain |
|----------|---------|
| 1 | A [minotaur](/bestiary/monstrosity/minotaur-of-baphomet-xmm.md) captures and devours other creatures limb by limb as part of a bizarre ritual honoring Mogis. |
| 2 | A tribe of [berserkers](/bestiary/humanoid/berserker-xmm.md) ride [giant bats](/bestiary/beast/giant-bat-xmm.md) and hunt humanoids that travel through the canyon. |
| 3 | An [adult blue dragon](/bestiary/dragon/adult-blue-dragon-xmm.md) enslaves the inhabitants of the caves, intent on building an army to conquer a neighboring settlement. |
| 4 | A [manticore](/bestiary/monstrosity/manticore-xmm.md) captures warriors and forces them to fight to the death for its amusement. |
| 5 | A group of [cyclopes](/bestiary/giant/cyclops-sentry-xmm.md) capture travelers and keep them as livestock in their caves. |
| 6 | A [lamia](/bestiary/fiend/lamia-xmm.md) lures young nobles into its cult with promises of hedonism and sells those who disappoint it to other canyon inhabitants. |
| 7 | A vicious [gynosphinx](/bestiary/celestial/sphinx-of-lore-xmm.md) captures groups of people, then forces one person to choose which of the others should be devoured first. |
| 8 | The victims of the canyon's inhabitants rise as [wraiths](/bestiary/undead/wraith-xmm.md) determined to end all life in the area. |
