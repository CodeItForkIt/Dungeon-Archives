# More Adventure Hooks!; Dankwood Adventure Hooks
*Source: Muk's Guide To Everything He Learned From Tasha p. 31* 

**Dankwood Adventure Hooks**

| dice: d8 | Adventure Hook |
|----------|----------------|
| 1 | A flute, that when played, summons a [pegasus](/bestiary/celestial/pegasus-xmm.md). (Where does the [pegasus](/bestiary/celestial/pegasus-xmm.md) fly to if you get on its back?) |
| 2 | A carved wooden bird that comes to life when picked up and leads the adventurers to the entrance of a cave. (What treasures are in the cave?) |
| 3 | A [pixie](/bestiary/fey/pixie-xmm.md) flies up to you and asks if you would help to chase a [troll](/bestiary/giant/troll-xmm.md) away from its village. (How do you get rid of a dangerous troll?) |
| 4 | A hunter is in the forest looking for a rare elk with golden antlers. (Either help the hunter catch the elk or help the elk escape the hunter.) |
| 5 | An elf who claims to be of royal blood is seeking refuge in the Dankwood. He is being hunted by rival elves and is willing to pay gold for your help. (Is the elf really who he says he is? Who are the rival elves?) |
| 6 | A gnome rushes up to you, hands you a golden key, and tells you, "Find the crystal cave and save me." before turning to stone! |
| 7 | One of your nets traps a [tiger](/bestiary/beast/tiger-xmm.md). As you are figuring out what to do with it, the [tiger](/bestiary/beast/tiger-xmm.md) speaks and claims that it was once a halfling that was cursed by an evil wizard. |
| 8 | A bounty hunter is on the lookout for a criminal. Is the criminal actually bad or is the bounty hunter bad? |
