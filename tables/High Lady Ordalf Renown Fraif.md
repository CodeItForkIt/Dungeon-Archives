# High Lady Ordalf Renown
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 184* 

**High Lady Ordalf Renown**

| Renown Score | Reward |
|--------------|--------|
| 3+ | You are welcome in Ordalf's court. Fey in Sarifal are [Friendly](/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 5 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 5, Ordalf gifts you a random Common magic item. |
| 10+ | Fey on Gwynneth are [Friendly](/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 15 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 15, Ordalf gifts you either a random Rare magic item or a pet [Blink Dog](/bestiary/fey/blink-dog-xmm.md) (your choice). |
| 30+ | You can use any fairy trod located in the Moonshae Isles. Fey in the Moonshaes are [Friendly](/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
