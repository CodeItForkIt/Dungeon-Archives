# Forest Shrine Villains
*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 158* 

**Forest Shrine Villains**

| dice: d6 | Villain |
|----------|---------|
| 1 | A group of [dryads](/bestiary/fey/dryad-xmm.md), enraged by seeing a section of forest burned, plots to destroy the shrine in order to punish the druids for failing to protect the trees. |
| 2 | Poachers ([scouts](/bestiary/humanoid/scout-xmm.md)) kill the shrine's defenders and set their traps in the surrounding forest. |
| 3 | The shrine stands in the path of a [hydra](/bestiary/monstrosity/hydra-xmm.md) on a destructive rampage. |
| 4 | A [druid](/bestiary/humanoid/druid-xmm.md) who lost her spouse in a hunting accident attacks any hunters who come to the shrine. |
| 5 | A [giant boar](/bestiary/beast/giant-boar-xmm.md) affected by the [awaken](/spells/awaken-xphb.md) spell convinces the shrine's druids to cast the spell on other boars to strike back at a contingent of hunting nobles. |
| 6 | A [cult fanatic](/bestiary/humanoid/cultist-fanatic-xmm.md) of Erebos infiltrates the shrine in disguise and plans to kill all its plant life. |
