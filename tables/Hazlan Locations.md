# Hazlan Locations
*Source: RHW* 

**Hazlan Locations**

| Location | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Balsam Wood | A plant at the center of the dense Balsam Wood can cure any illness. Unnervingly, the forest is alive, aware, and prone to agony, screaming if someone disturbs so much as a leaf. |
| The Brew | Alchemical pollution from the village of Ramulai has poisoned the toxic marsh now known as the Brew. Creeks and bogs often erupt with violent fungal creatures and magical effects. |
| Fallen Sisters | The Fallen Sisters are three mountains that have collapsed into triangular, bottomless pits. Vistani often camp here. |
| Fleshless Forest | Plants and creatures in the Fleshless Forest were turned to stone. The condition is infectious. |
| The Lacuna | The Lacuna is a region of knee-high mists that reach toward places of powerful magic, such as Hazlik's palace, Veneficus. Anyone who steps into these mists vanishes, and few reemerge. |
| The Marks | The sigils of Hazlik's mightiest apprentices mark the land in giant, magic-enhancing geoglyphs. The Lacuna grows toward these Marks—and a fourth, mysterious glyph. |
| Moonstone Valley | Meteors and otherworldly creatures crash into this wasteland during every new moon. |
| Mound of the Worm | Earth from deep underground rose to form the Mound of the Worm mesa, unleashing dozens of [Purple Worms](/bestiary/monstrosity/purple-worm-xmm.md). The aged, albino worm Gravedrinker dominates this region. |
| Orbitoclasts | The Orbitoclasts are floating rock formations that resemble lobotomy tools. Their pointed ends align toward the nearest magic-user. |
| Philosopher's Egg | A cult of magic-warped beings worships an eight-story, citrine egg. The cult searches for a prophet who's never been touched by magic to release the antimagic entity within. |
| The Playas | Former lakes, the Playas are now quicksand-riddled [Purple Worm](/bestiary/monstrosity/purple-worm-xmm.md) feeding grounds. |
| Ramulai | Mines near the village of Ramulai yield rare magical elements. Refineries process these volatile resources, but in so doing, they magically sicken the people and the land. |
| Seething | The lifeless Seething region crumbles away into the Mists. A heavy footfall can break its surface. |
