# Dementlieu Locations
*Source: RHW* 

**Dementlieu Locations**

| Location | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Bonniville Museum of the Sea | Green windows tint this museum's collection of aquatic oddities and preserved sea creatures in drowned light. A display of the wolfish masthead of the S.S. Bonniville is said to be cursed. |
| D'Honaire Estate | The home of [Saidra d'Honaire](/bestiary/npc/saidra-dhonaire-rhw.md) and the site of her Grand Masquerades is accessible only by the nobles' stylish gondolas or larger ships (see "Saidra's Lair"). |
| Great Library | The Great Library's supposedly peerless collection houses fewer than a thousand titles, but hundreds of copies of each. A few rare tomes hide among the unremarkable copies. |
| House of Wax | Alexandre du Cire's House of Wax displays wax copies of nobles and celebrities. In the basements, those who've been replaced by lifelike wax constructs remain imprisoned. |
| Lord Governor's Manor | The lord governor's home is the largest and most ornate in the city. The staff maintains only a portion of the estate, as the rest is said to be haunted by the city's former rulers. |
| Mother of Tears Cathedral | The Ezra-worshipping clergy of the grandiose Mother of Tears Cathedral weep for all the suffering and horror of the world but take no action to cure or combat it. |
| Palace of Enlightenment | The seat of Port-a-Lucine's government is filled with empty council chambers. A few caretakers mind the gardens and the collections of art, civil records, and architectural plans. |
| Phlegethan Hospital | A patient with a preternatural ability to bend others to his will has taken over the Phlegethan Hospital. He controls its doctors and claims to be the rightful Duke d'Honaire. |
| Port-a-Lucine Opera House | The opera is Port-a-Lucine's hub of culture and boasts ornate galleries, but the backstage areas are a labyrinth where crew members occasionally go missing—victims of fierce feuds. |
| Red Widow Theater | A sculpture of a giant crimson spider looms over the lively Red Widow Theater. Patrons seem undeterred by stories of shape-shifting spiders that hunt the theater's clientele. |
| Three Odd Gables | A coven of godmotherly [Green Hags](/bestiary/fey/green-hag-xmm.md) lives in three crooked houses on Mill Street. The hags fulfill their guests' dreams, but their contracts always have a hidden catch. |
| University of Dementlieu | The University of Dementlieu is largely occupied by narcissistic nobles and their fatuous students. Some true scholars conduct actual research hidden from their clout-chasing peers. |
| Zuvich Hospital | Zuvich Hospital offers skilled, science-based medical care. Dr. Doloros Zuvich recognizes that some of her patients' accounts describe encounters with supernatural beings. |
