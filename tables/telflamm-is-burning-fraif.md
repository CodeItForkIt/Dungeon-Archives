# Telflamm Is Burning
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 30* 

| dice: 1d4 | Encounter |
|-----------|-----------|
| 1 | A dozen rioters ([Commoners](/bestiary/humanoid/commoner-xmm.md)) mistake an innocent for a Shadowmaster informer and attempt vigilante justice. |
| 2 | A human populist agitator named Gehlen Kriska (Medium [Performer Maestro](/bestiary/humanoid/performer-maestro-xmm.md)), rallies the mob, blaming the city council for the crisis. If the characters defend the council or try to disperse the mob, Gehlen turns the mob's anger on the party. |
| 3 | Rioters have set a building on fire. Spectators watch helplessly as `1d4` victims cry for help from within the blaze. Use the "Manor House map" from appendix B of the "Dungeon Master's Guide", scattering victims on each floor. Use the rules for an inferno in [chapter 3](/traps-hazards/inferno-xdmg.md) of the Dungeon Master's Guide to adjudicate the fire. |
| 4 | A Shadowmaster [Assassin](/bestiary/humanoid/assassin-xmm.md) targets the characters, approaching stealthily and targeting spellcasters and other lightly armored characters first. |
^1-encounter
