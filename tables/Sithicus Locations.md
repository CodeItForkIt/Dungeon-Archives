# Sithicus Locations
*Source: RHW* 

**Sithicus Locations**

| Location | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Giant's Cloak | Throughout the Giant's Cloak forest are petrified trees carved with strange runes and weeping tar-like sap. Stone giant hermits tend these groves and set deadly traps against trespassers. |
| Har-Thelen | Har-Thelen is largely abandoned. Its gates stand open, and only a few hundred people reside at its center. By night, stray shadows work and live among the abandoned structures. |
| Hroth | The decrepit town of Hroth is built amid giant swamp trees, many of which lean at precarious angles. The bogs nearby are said to be magic and can return to life those buried within. |
| Iron Warden Inn | The Iron Warden Inn is closed by day, but at night, those with enough wealth to buy entry gamble at games of cards and death's-head dice. Sometimes the dead come to play. |
| Kendralind | A hidden village of faded tree houses, huts, and burrows, Kendralind is home to a few dozen kender vampires. The residents are equally delighted by games, whimsical tales, and bloodshed. |
| Lake of Glass | The Lake of Glass moves like water but is as solid as glass. At night, the lake reflects the hidden moon, Nuitari, and magic cast within its reflected light is empowered but cursed. |
| Mal-Erek | Forgotten scholars built Mal-Erek amid giant trees. Now only squatters and obsessed scholars reside here, with few noticing the decay of the vaults and archives around them. |
| Merchant's Slash | The Merchant's Slash trail is littered with the wreckage of merchant and traveler caravans. Some are still occupied by eccentric traders selling eerie wares from strange lands. |
| Nedragaard Keep | The towering fortress of Lord Soth is strangled by black thorns and blazes with cataclysmic flame. Within, undead knights and spectral elves reenact and recite Soth's great failures. |
| Sorrow | A hidden prison for the living guarded by the dead, Sorrow accepts mysterious transfers from beyond the Mists. Many thought dead in other domains are imprisoned here. |
| Veldrava | A dismal work camp stands at the entrance to a maze of unfathomably deep salt mines. Caustic minerals burn explorers who seek the source of whispers from the depths. |
