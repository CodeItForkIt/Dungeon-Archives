# Falkovnia Locations
*Source: RHW* 

**Falkovnia Locations**

| Location | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Aerie | Swarms of zombified birds of prey attack any living creatures near the village of Aerie, especially those who use the cable ferries crossing the Archer River. |
| Delmunster | Zombies avoid the idyllic village of Delmunster. People say that it is protected by a fallen star; Few suspect the [Bodytaker Plant](/bestiary/plant/bodytaker-plant-rhw.md) that has claimed the community. |
| Fort Watten | A force of Talons under the command of Major Olana Crepeau was ordered to guard Falkovnia's southern border at Fort Watten. No one in Lekar has heard from them for moons. |
| Hatzimvas Hospital | The doctors of the secluded Hatzimvas Hospital seek a medical or supernatural cure to zombification. Mysteriously, Drakov allows the hospital to work unimpeded. |
| Morfenzi | The ruins of Morfenzi hide a Talon outpost. Here the amoral scientist Vjorn Horstman conducts monstrous experiments, creating living weapons using "primal serum." |
| Silbervas | Fractious bands of survivors sworn to silence scavenge the ruined city of Silbervas. They avoid the ominously lit Dekovan Palace, which overlooks the ruins. |
| Stangengrad | The silent, scarred spirits of commoners and Talons haunt the ruined fortress-city of Stangengrad. Vladeska forbids entry to the city so no one can learn of the atrocity that took place there. |
| Stonespine Mountains | The knight Gondegal leads rebels opposed to the Talons. Her stronghold is said to be hidden in the Stonespine Mountains, but none who join Gondegal are ever seen again. |
| Three Traitors | Between the three lakes known as the Three Traitors is a muddy patch where Vistani often camp. These travelers have a knack for avoiding Talon patrols. |
| Vigila Forest | In the dense Vigila Forest stand towering black trees called Sentries of Death. Tales say the wood is haunted and these trees' roots reach into the realm of the dead. |
| Willow Watch | The hastily constructed Willow Watch tower was built on the ruins of a church of Ezra. The Talons here report strange lights in the nearby forest and sounds from the catacombs below. |
