# Jarl Olfsvenn Renown
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Adventures in Faerûn p. 184* 

**Jarl Olfsvenn Renown**

| Renown Score | Reward |
|--------------|--------|
| 3+ | You are welcome in Olfsvenn's court, and members of the court are [Friendly](/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 5+ | You can hire hirelings from Olfsvenn's court for half cost. |
| 10 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 10, Olfsvenn rewards you with 100 GP worth of gemstones and art objects. |
| 15+ | Norlanders in the Moonshae Isles are [Friendly](/rules/variant-rules/friendly-attitude-xphb.md) to you by default. |
| 20 | The first time your Renown Score reaches 20, Olfsvenn gifts you a Norlander Longship (see "Moonshae Vehicles" earlier in the chapter). |
| 30+ | You can request a Norlander contingent of four [Guards](/bestiary/humanoid/guard-xmm.md) to help you for one mission. Once you use this reward, you can't do so again until your Renown Score increases. |
