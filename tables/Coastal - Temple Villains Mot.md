# Coastal Temple Villains
*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 174* 

**Coastal Temple Villains**

| dice: d6 | Villain |
|----------|---------|
| 1 | A [kraken](/bestiary/monstrosity/kraken-xmm.md) threatens to destroy the temple if worshipers don't pay tribute to the creature. |
| 2 | A [Naiad](/bestiary/fey/naiad-mot.md) (see "chapter 6") hides inside the temple and murders fishers who worship here for their crimes against the sea. |
| 3 | A group of sirens (see the [harpies](/bestiary/monstrosity/harpy-xmm.md) entry in "chapter 6") take over the temple and lure new victims inside to drown themselves during high tide. |
| 4 | A [priest](/bestiary/humanoid/priest-xmm.md) of Thassa falsely convinces elderly worshipers that giving more wealth to the temple increases the length of time they live. |
| 5 | A [giant octopus](/bestiary/beast/giant-octopus-xmm.md) under the effects of the [awaken](/spells/awaken-xphb.md) spell hunts in the temple at high tide. |
| 6 | A rageful [triton master of waves](/bestiary/humanoid/triton-master-of-waves-mot.md) (see "chapter 6") believes everyone in a temple is secretly serving a kraken and begins murdering them. |
