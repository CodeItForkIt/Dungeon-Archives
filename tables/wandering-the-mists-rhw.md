# Wandering the Mists
*Source: RHW* 

**Wandering the Mists**

| dice: 1d100 | Characters Emerge from the Mists... |
|-------------|-------------------------------------|
| 01–20 | In a domain of your choosing. |
| 21–40 | After wandering for another `1d6` hours and rolling again on this table. |
| 41–65 | `1d100` feet from where they entered. |
| 66–75 | A mile from where they entered at midnight of the following night. |
| 76–85 | Inside a structure within the domain they tried to leave, perhaps a cave, crypt, shed, or closet. |
| 86–95 | After encountering creatures within the Mists. Roll any die. If you roll an even number, a terrified [Commoner](/bestiary/humanoid/commoner-xmm.md) appears. If you roll an odd number, a [Mist Horror](/bestiary/undead/mist-horror-rhw.md) (see chapter 5) appears. Roll again on this table. |
| 96–99 | Where they entered, only to find the domain changed. Perhaps someone the characters knew is gone and no one remembers the person. |
| 100 | On a world on the Material Plane. After `1d6` hours, the Mists rise around them once more and return them to the domain they departed. |
