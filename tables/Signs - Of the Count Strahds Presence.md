# Signs of the Count; Strahd's Presence
*Source: RHW* 

**Strahd's Presence**

| dice: 1d8 | Strahd's Presence Is Suggested... |
|-----------|-----------------------------------|
| 1 | As clouds momentarily resemble clawed hands, gaping skulls, or Castle Ravenloft. |
| 2 | As wolves or ravens soundlessly follow the characters and mirror their actions. |
| 3 | By faded carvings or tapestries bearing the von Zarovich crest. |
| 4 | During a tarokka reading, when the Darklord card changes to resemble Strahd. |
| 5 | In a dream where a character becomes charmed or threatened by Strahd. |
| 6 | In a rustic shrine littered with vials of dried blood, snapped splinters, and wolf fangs. |
| 7 | When characters awake to find swarms of roosting bats or bloodless animal corpses. |
| 8 | When spying villagers try to eavesdrop on or steal small things from the characters. |
