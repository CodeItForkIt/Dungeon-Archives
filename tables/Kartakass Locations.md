# Kartakass Locations
*Source: RHW* 

**Kartakass Locations**

| Location | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Argent Falls Conservatory | Performers flock to a bardic college atop the Argent Falls. Caverns behind the falls bear rich silver deposits but are haunted by [Banshees](/bestiary/undead/banshee-xmm.md)—the souls of failed performers. |
| The Catacombs | Violent goblins lurk amid mazelike canyons called the Catacombs. Here, the goblin gladiator Nym Pymplee hosts fighting tournaments, with victors winning treasures from distant domains. |
| Cavea Plains | The Cavea Plains' acoustics carry voices over great distances. Any speech above a whisper might attract the attention of [wolves](/bestiary/beast/wolf-xmm.md). |
| Cesca's Vantage | On the night of a full moon, a tarokka-reading wolf appears atop Cesca's Vantage. She produces crimson cards from any corpse she is offered, and her readings are never wrong. |
| Cortton | A plague has taken root in Cortton. The town's residents cake heavy makeup and garish blush on their faces, desperate to convince themselves and others that they're uninfected. |
| Emherst | Every "resident" of Emherst is an in-character improvisational actor. They live in the Understage, an underground network of backstages, and they never interrupt performances. |
| Goblyn Graves | Undead goblinoids often emerge from the Goblyn Graves, the former lair of Radaga, an undead witch said to possess powerful magic treasures. |
| House of Illusions | This fun house of visual illusions is run by Jack Bequick: a vampire cursed with eternal invisibility and a twisted sense of humor. |
| Medria | Several dramatic-effects artists call the fishing village of Medria home. Colorful explosions and wandering puppets are commonplace. |
| Point Hallucination | The Mists often surge over Lake Variatio to deposit newcomers at the rickety quay known as Point Hallucination, where traveling merchants and performers eagerly greet them. |
| Wildersung Wood | At twilight, a sourceless chorus drifts through these woods. Those who don't sing along risk losing their lives, while those who discover the last verse discover a hidden sanctuary. |
