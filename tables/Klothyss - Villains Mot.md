# Klothys's Villains
*Source: Mythic Odysseys of Theros p. 144* 

**Klothys's Villains**

| dice: d6 | Villain |
|----------|---------|
| 1 | A single-minded Nyxborn [assassin](/bestiary/humanoid/assassin-xmm.md) of Klothys hunts a hero who was raised from the dead. |
| 2 | Believing in the holiness of a terrible beast, Klothys's [acolytes](/bestiary/humanoid/priest-acolyte-xmm.md) murder heroes sent to deal with it. |
| 3 | Certain that civilization offends fate, a [priest](/bestiary/humanoid/priest-xmm.md) raises a herd of [typhons](/bestiary/monstrosity/typhon-mot.md) to release on a city. |
| 4 | Insulted [satyr revelers](/bestiary/fey/satyr-reveler-mot.md) (see "chapter 6") channel Klothys's magic to drive a polis's priests into embarrassing debauchery. |
| 5 | An [oracle](/bestiary/humanoid/oracle-mot.md) (see "chapter 6") knows it is a character's destiny to serve Klothys. The seer manufactures tragedy for the individual to provoke this revelation. |
| 6 | A [cult fanatic](/bestiary/humanoid/cultist-fanatic-xmm.md) of Klothys believes that the characters' deeds mark them as servants of the titans, destined to free one of those ancient terrors. |
