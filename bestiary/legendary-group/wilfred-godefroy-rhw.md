# Wilfred Godefroy

## Regional Effects


Mordent is altered by Lord Godefroy's presence, creating the following effects:

- **Closing the Borders.** While in his domain, Lord Godefroy can close or open the borders to Mordent. While the borders are closed, the groans and whispers of the dead fill the Mists surrounding Mordent. Creatures in the Mists have the [Deafened](/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition.  
- **Ears Everywhere.** While in his domain, Lord Godefroy can cast [Sending](/spells/sending-xphb.md), requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability. When casting the spell in this way, Lord Godefroy can target any Undead creature in Mordent, in addition to the usual targets.  
- **Spectral Serfs.** A Humanoid that dies in Mordent rises 7 days later as a [Specter](/bestiary/undead/specter-xmm.md) under Lord Godefroy's control, unless the Humanoid is restored to life.  

If Wilfred dies, these effects end immediately.
