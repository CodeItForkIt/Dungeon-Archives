# Vampire Infernalist

## Regional Effects


The region containing an infernalist's lair is warped by its presence, creating the following effects, all of which happen at the vampire's discretion:

- **Diabolic Beasts.** Animals in the vampire's domain serve the vampire's will. From dusk until dawn, Medium or smaller Beasts have the Charmed condition while within 1 mile of the lair.  
- **Drained Essence.** Grasping shadows within 1 mile of the lair sap the body and mind. Creatures (excluding the vampire and its allies) that finish a Short or Long Rest while within 1 mile of the lair make a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, a creature can't spend Hit Point Dice at the end of the rest and doesn't regain Hit Points, Hit Point Dice, or spell slots at the end of the rest.  
- **Tenacious Lore.** Within 1 mile of the lair, flame doesn't burn written material.  

If the infernalist dies or moves its lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
