# Karas Chembryl

## Regional Effects


Karas extends her connection to the Dead Three into the region she inhabits, creating the following effects:

- **Creeping Dread.** Terror runs rampant in Karas's domain. Within 1 mile of the lair, creatures other than members of the Zhentarim and cultists of Bane, Bhaal, or Myrkul have Disadvantage on saving throws to avoid or end the Frightened condition.  
- **Death's Lore.** When a Humanoid dies within 1 mile of the lair, Karas learns one secret that the Humanoid knew.  

If Karas dies or moves her lair elsewhere, these effects end immediately.
