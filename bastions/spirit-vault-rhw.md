# Spirit Vault
*Level 13 Bastion facility*  

- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Space**: Roomy (16 sq)
- **Hirelings**: 1
- **Order**: research

A Spirit Vault is magically warded to imprison and exorcise malevolent spirits and other supernatural forces. Haunted portraits, cursed relics, and other eerie curios line the shelves of this dark vault. This facility always counts as Haunted.

## Esoteric Knowledge

After spending a Long Rest in your Spirit Vault, you gain one of the following magical Charms:

- [Charm of Exorcism](/rewards/charm-of-exorcism-rhw.md)  
- [Charm of Malediction](/rewards/charm-of-malediction-rhw.md)  

The Charm lasts for 7 days or until you use it. Intelligence, Wisdom, or Charisma is your spellcasting ability for the Charm's spells (choose each time you gain this Charm).

## Research: Ask the Occult

When you issue the Research order to this facility, you or its hireling study the various relics and question the imprisoned entities within your Spirit Vault. This work takes 7 days. At the end of this time, roll `1d6`. On a 1, the work is psychically taxing; if you did the research, you take `6d6` Psychic damage, and if the hireling did the research, they immediately die, and this facility can't be used again until your next Bastion turn. Otherwise, you or the hireling gain information as if you had successfully cast the [Contact Other Plane](/spells/contact-other-plane-xphb.md) spell. The hireling shares any information they learned with you the next time you speak.
