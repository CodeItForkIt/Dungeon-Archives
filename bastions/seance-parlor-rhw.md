# Séance Parlor
*Level 5 Bastion facility*  

- **Prerequisites**: None
- **Space**: Cramped (4 sq)
- **Hirelings**: 1
- **Order**: research

The veil between the world of the living and the dead grows thin in a Séance Parlor.

## Research: Fortune Telling

When you issue the Research order to this facility, you commission its hireling to read your fortune by casting bones, interpreting tea leaves, performing a tarokka reading, or using some other means. After your fortune is read, you gain [Heroic Inspiration](/rules/variant-rules/heroic-inspiration-xphb.md). You can't gain this benefit again until you finish a Long Rest.

## Speak with Dead

After spending a Long Rest in your Séance Parlor, you can cast [Speak with Dead](/spells/speak-with-dead-xphb.md) immediately without expending a spell slot or needing Material components. When you cast it in this way, you can target any deceased creature whose name you know, as long as you have a piece of its remains (such as ashes or a bone) or an object that was important to the creature in life (such as a brooch or signature weapon). The target speaks through an implement in the Séance Room such as a crystal ball or spirit board.
