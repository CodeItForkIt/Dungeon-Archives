# Trebuchet
*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 97*  

A Trebuchet is a catapult that throws its payload in a high arc so it can hit targets behind walls. Loading a Trebuchet requires two [Utilize](/rules/actions.md#Utilize) actions, and aiming it requires two more [Utilize](/rules/actions.md#Utilize) actions. Then a crew member can take the Trebuchet Stone action.
