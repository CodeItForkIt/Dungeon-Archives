# Vampire Survivor
*Source: Astarion's Book of Hungers p. 7*  


- **Ability Scores.** Dexterity, Constitution, Wisdom  
- **Feat.** [Vampire Hunter](/feats/vampire-hunter-abh.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Insight](/rules/skills.md#Insight) and [Religion](/rules/skills.md#Religion)  
- **Tool Proficiency.** [Woodcarver's Tools](/items/woodcarvers-tools-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Woodcarver's Tools](/items/woodcarvers-tools-xphb.md), [Crowbar](/items/crowbar-xphb.md), [Hooded Lantern](/items/hooded-lantern-xphb.md), [Holy Symbol](/items/holy-symbol-xphb.md) ([reliquary](/items/reliquary-xphb.md)), [Holy Water](/items/holy-water-xphb.md), [Mirror](/items/mirror-xphb.md), [Oil](/items/oil-xphb.md) (3 flasks), [Tinderbox](/items/tinderbox-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), [Waterskin](/items/waterskin-xphb.md), 4 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

You witnessed or survived a vampire attack. You might have been directly involved in this confrontation, or perhaps you were frozen in terror by what you saw. Regardless, you remain vigilant for monster attacks and pride yourself on being ready for anything. No vampire will catch you or your allies by surprise again.
