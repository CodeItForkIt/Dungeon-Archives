# Entertainer
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 180*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/backgrounds/XPHB/Entertainer.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Strength, Dexterity, Charisma  
- **Feat.** [Musician](/feats/musician-xphb.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Acrobatics](/rules/skills.md#Acrobatics), [Performance](/rules/skills.md#Performance)  
- **Tool Proficiency.** Choose one kind of [Musical Instrument](/items/musical-instrument-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Musical Instrument](/items/musical-instrument-xphb.md) (same as above), [2 Costumes](/items/costume-xphb.md), [Mirror](/items/mirror-xphb.md), [Perfume](/items/perfume-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), 11 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

You spent much of your youth following roving fairs and carnivals, performing odd jobs for musicians and acrobats in exchange for lessons. You may have learned how to walk a tightrope, how to play a [lute](/items/lute-xphb.md) in a distinct style, or how to recite poetry with impeccable diction. To this day, you thrive on applause and long for the stage.
