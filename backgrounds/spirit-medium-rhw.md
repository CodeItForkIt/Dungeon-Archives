# Spirit Medium
*Source: RHW*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/backgrounds/RHW/Spirit%20Medium.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom  
- **Feat.** A Dark Gift feat of your choice ([Gathered Whispers](/feats/gathered-whispers-rhw.md) is recommended)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Insight](/rules/skills.md#Insight) and [Religion](/rules/skills.md#Religion)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** Choose one kind of [Gaming Set](/items/gaming-set-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Dagger](/items/dagger-xphb.md), [Gaming Set](/items/gaming-set-xphb.md) (same as above), [Basket](/items/basket-xphb.md), [Bell](/items/bell-xphb.md), 8 Candles, [Ink](/items/ink-xphb.md), [Ink](/items/ink-xphb.md) Pen, [Paper](/items/paper-xphb.md) (5 sheets), [Tinderbox](/items/tinderbox-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), and 32 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

Through strange rituals and fateful encounters, you've discovered that you bear a unique connection to the spirits of the dead and damned. Your body is a conduit for such spirits, granting you prescient insight from beyond. But these spirits' intentions aren't always innocent, and their gifts come at a haunting cost.
