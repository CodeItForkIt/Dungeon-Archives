# Moonwell Pilgrim
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 33*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/backgrounds/FRHoF/Moonwell%20Pilgrim.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Constitution, Wisdom, Charisma  
- **Feat.** [Magic Initiate](/feats/magic-initiate-xphb.md) (Druid)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Nature](/rules/skills.md#Nature) and [Performance](/rules/skills.md#Performance)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** [Painter's Supplies](/items/painters-supplies-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Quarterstaff](/items/quarterstaff-xphb.md), [Painter's Supplies](/items/painters-supplies-xphb.md), [Bedroll](/items/bedroll-xphb.md), [Bell](/items/bell-xphb.md), [Pouch](/items/pouch-xphb.md), [Robe](/items/robe-xphb.md), [String](/items/string-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), [Waterskin](/items/waterskin-xphb.md), 38 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

Like many who hail from the Moonshae Isles, you grew up revering the blessed land, its unique gods, and the mysterious shrines called the moonwells. As a moonwell pilgrim, you undertook a quest to visit and commune with every moonwell on (or off) the map. Along your idyllic journeys, you collected a repertoire of Moonshavian folk songs, painted landscapes of enchanting vistas, and even learned how to wield a bit of primal magic.
