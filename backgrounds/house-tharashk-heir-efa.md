# House Tharashk Heir
*Source: Eberron: Forge of the Artificer p. 32*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/backgrounds/EFA/House%20Tharashk%20Heir.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom  
- **Feat.** [Mark of Finding](/feats/mark-of-finding-efa.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Perception](/rules/skills.md#Perception) and [Survival](/rules/skills.md#Survival)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** Choose one kind of [Gaming Set](/items/gaming-set-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Gaming Set](/items/gaming-set-xphb.md) (same as above), [Climber's Kit](/items/climbers-kit-xphb.md), [Fine Clothes](/items/fine-clothes-xphb.md), [Hunting Trap](/items/hunting-trap-xphb.md), [Manacles](/items/manacles-xphb.md), 2 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

Heirs of other houses lead lives of luxury, but in House Tharashk you learned self-reliance from an early age. What your young house lacks in resources, it makes up for in spirit and determination. Tharashk has a destiny to fulfill, and you have a part in it. Whether in combat or in social interaction, you have learned to play the role of the hunter: study your enemies, exploit their weaknesses, and do whatever it takes to achieve victory.
