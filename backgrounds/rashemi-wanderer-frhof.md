# Rashemi Wanderer
*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 35*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/backgrounds/FRHoF/Rashemi%20Wanderer.webp#right)

- **Ability Scores.** Strength, Constitution, Charisma  
- **Feat.** [Tough](/feats/tough-xphb.md)  
- **Skill Proficiencies.** [Intimidation](/rules/skills.md#Intimidation) and [Perception](/rules/skills.md#Perception)  
- **Tool Proficiencies.** [Cartographer's Tools](/items/cartographers-tools-xphb.md)  
- **Equipment.** Choose A or B: (A) [Cartographer's Tools](/items/cartographers-tools-xphb.md), [Backpack](/items/backpack-xphb.md), [Bedroll](/items/bedroll-xphb.md), [Hooded Lantern](/items/hooded-lantern-xphb.md), [Oil](/items/oil-xphb.md) (3 flasks), [Rope](/items/rope-xphb.md), [Tinderbox](/items/tinderbox-xphb.md), [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md), [Waterskin](/items/waterskin-xphb.md), 23 GP; or (B) 50 GP  

You spent years wandering the highlands of Rashemen, a dangerous windswept heath that's dotted with ancient obelisks enchanted to imprison Fiends and home to dragons, gnolls, and other deadly creatures. Friendships are hard to find in such an isolated land, and you've learned to keep strangers at a distance.
