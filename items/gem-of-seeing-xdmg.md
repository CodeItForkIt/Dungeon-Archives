# Gem of Seeing
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Gem%20of%20Seeing.webp#right)


This gem has 3 charges. As a [Magic](/rules/actions.md#Magic) action, you can expend 1 charge. For the next 10 minutes, you have [Truesight](/rules/senses.md#Truesight) out to 120 feet when you peer through the gem.

The gem regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 264. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
