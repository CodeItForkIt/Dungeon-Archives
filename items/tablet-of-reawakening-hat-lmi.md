# Tablet of Reawakening
*Wondrous item, legendary*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/HAT-LMI/Tablet%20of%20Reawakening.webp#right)


Witches of Rashemen created this Tiny stone tablet to counter the necromancy of the Red Wizards. As an action, you can cast [true resurrection](/spells/true-resurrection-xphb.md) from the tablet. Once used in this way, the tablet turns to dust and is destroyed.

*Source: Honor Among Thieves: Legendary Magic Items*
