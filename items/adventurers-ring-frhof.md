# Adventurer's Ring
*Ring, common*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/FRHoF/Adventurer%27s%20Ring.webp#right)


Lamps are a liability on adventures. Delicate and prone to oil spillage, lamps fill a hand that should otherwise be wielding a weapon or casting a spell. Once you try one of our elegant and practical rings, you'll never go back to cumbersome flint and fire.

While the cover on this ring is open, the ring produces a flame that creates no heat and consumes no fuel. It sheds [Bright Light](/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 20-foot radius and [Dim Light](/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 20 feet. As a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can close the cover, smothering the flame, or open it again.

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
