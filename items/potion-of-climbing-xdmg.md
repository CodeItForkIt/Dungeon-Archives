# Potion of Climbing
*Potion, common*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Potion%20of%20Climbing.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

When you drink this potion, you gain a [Climb Speed](/rules/variant-rules/climb-speed-xphb.md) equal to your [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) for 1 hour. During this time, you have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Strength ([Athletics](/rules/skills.md#Athletics)) checks to climb.

This potion is separated into brown, silver, and gray layers resembling bands of stone. Shaking the bottle fails to mix the colors.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 287. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
