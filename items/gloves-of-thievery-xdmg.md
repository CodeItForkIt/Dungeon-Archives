# Gloves of Thievery
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Gloves%20of%20Thievery.webp#right)


These gloves are imperceptible while worn. While wearing them, you gain a +5 bonus to Dexterity ([Sleight of Hand](/rules/skills.md#Sleight%20of%20Hand)) checks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 265. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
