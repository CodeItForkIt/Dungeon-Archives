# Boots of Elvenkind
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Boots%20of%20Elvenkind.webp#right)


While you wear these boots, your steps make no sound, regardless of the surface you are moving across. You also have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on Dexterity ([Stealth](/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) checks.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 239. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
