# Tidecaller Trident
*Weapon ([trident](/items/trident-xphb.md)), very rare (requires attunement)*  


- **Damage**:
  - One-handed: 1d6 piercing
  - Two-handed: 1d8 piercing
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Thrown](/rules/item-properties.md#Thrown), [Versatile](/rules/item-properties.md#Versatile)
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic trident. You also have advantage on attack rolls made with this weapon while underwater.

This trident has 3 charges. You can expend 1 charge to cast [Control Water](/spells/control-water-xphb.md) (save DC 15) from the trident or 3 charges to cast [Tsunami](/spells/tsunami-xphb.md) (save DC 15) from it instead. The trident regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 69*
