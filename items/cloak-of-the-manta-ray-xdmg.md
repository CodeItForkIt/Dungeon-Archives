# Cloak of the Manta Ray
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Cloak%20of%20the%20Manta%20Ray.webp#right)
