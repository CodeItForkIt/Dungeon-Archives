# Hat of Disguise
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Hat%20of%20Disguise.webp#right)


While wearing this hat, you can cast the [Disguise Self](/spells/disguise-self-xphb.md) spell. The spell ends if the hat is removed.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 266. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
