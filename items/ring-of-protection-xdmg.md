# Ring of Protection
*Ring, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Ring%20of%20Protection.webp#right)


You gain a +1 bonus to [Armor Class](/rules/variant-rules/armor-class-xphb.md) and saving throws while wearing this ring.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 294. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
