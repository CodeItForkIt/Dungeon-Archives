# Hat of Vortexes
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  



This hat has 3 charges and regains all expended charges daily at dawn. You can take a [Magic](/rules/actions.md#Magic) action and expend 1 charge while holding the hat to release a magical vortex from it. The vortex fills a 10-foot [Cube](/rules/variant-rules/cube-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you and lasts for 1 hour. While the vortex is present, its area is [Difficult Terrain](/rules/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md).

You decide a vortex's visual details when you create it. For instance, the vortex might be multicolored or glittery.

*Source: Netheril's Fall*
