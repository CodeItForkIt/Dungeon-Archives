# Instrument of the Bards, Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern
*Wondrous item, instrument, uncommon (requires attunement by a bard)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Mac-Fuirmidh%20Cittern.webp#right)


An Instrument of the Bards is superior to an ordinary instrument in every way. Seven types of these instruments exist, each named after a bard college. A creature that attempts to play the instrument without being attuned to it must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or take `2d4` Psychic damage.

You can play the Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern to cast one of the following spells: [Fly](/spells/fly-xphb.md), [Invisibility](/spells/invisibility-xphb.md), [Levitate](/spells/levitate-xphb.md), [Protection from Evil and Good](/spells/protection-from-evil-and-good-xphb.md), [Barkskin](/spells/barkskin-xphb.md), [Cure Wounds](/spells/cure-wounds-xphb.md), and [Fog Cloud](/spells/fog-cloud-xphb.md). Once the Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern has been used to cast a spell, it can't be used to cast that spell again until the next dawn. The spells use your spellcasting ability and spell save DC.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 272*
