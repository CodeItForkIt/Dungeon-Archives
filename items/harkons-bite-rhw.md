# Harkon's Bite
*Wondrous item, cursed item, uncommon (requires attunement by a Humanoid)*  


- **Properties**: [Cursed](/rules/item-properties.md#Cursed%20Items)

A dire wolf's tooth dangles from this simple cord necklace. You gain a +1 bonus to ability checks and saving throws while you wear this necklace.

## Curse

This necklace is cursed. Attuning to the necklace curses you; this curse can't be removed until [Harkon Lukas](/bestiary/npc/harkon-lukas-rhw.md) dies. As long as you remain cursed, you become a [Werewolf](/bestiary/monstrosity/werewolf-xmm.md) serving Harkon Lukas under the DM's control during the night of a full moon.

*Source: RHW*
