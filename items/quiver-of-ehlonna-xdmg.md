# Quiver of Ehlonna
*Wondrous item, uncommon*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Quiver%20of%20Ehlonna.webp#right)

- **Weight**: 2.0 lbs.

Each of the quiver's three compartments connects to an extradimensional space that allows the quiver to hold numerous items while never weighing more than 2 pounds. The shortest compartment can hold up to 60 [Arrows](/items/arrow-xphb.md), [Bolts](/items/bolt-xphb.md), or similar objects. The midsize compartment holds up to 18 [Javelins](/items/javelin-xphb.md) or similar objects. The longest compartment holds up to 6 long objects, such as bows, [Quarterstaff's](/items/quarterstaff-xphb.md), or [Spears](/items/spear-xphb.md).

You can draw any item the quiver contains as if doing so from a regular quiver or scabbard.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 189. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> (as "Efficient Quiver") and the Free Rules (2024)*
