# Cloak of Many Fashions
*Wondrous item, common*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Cloak%20of%20Many%20Fashions.webp#right)


While wearing this cloak, you can take a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to change the style, color, and apparent quality of the garment. The cloak's weight doesn't change. Regardless of its appearance, the cloak can't be anything but a cloak. Although it can duplicate the appearance of other magic cloaks, it doesn't gain their magical properties.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 245*
