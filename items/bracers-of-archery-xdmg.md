# Bracers of Archery
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Bracers%20of%20Archery.webp#right)


While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the [Longbow](/items/longbow-xphb.md) and [Shortbow](/items/shortbow-xphb.md), and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls made with such weapons.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 240. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
