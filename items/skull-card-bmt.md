# Skull Card
*Wondrous item, legendary*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/decks/BMT/Deck%20of%20Many%20Things/19-skull.webp#right)


As an action, you can brandish this card and use it to cast [Spirit of Death](/spells/spirit-of-death-bmt.md) (see "chapter 7") as a 6th-level spell (spell attack bonus `+9`, save DC 17). Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

*Source: The Book of Many Things p. 15*
