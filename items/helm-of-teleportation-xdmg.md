# Helm of Teleportation
*Wondrous item, rare (requires attunement)*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Helm%20of%20Teleportation.webp#right)


This helm has 3 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Teleport](/spells/teleport-xphb.md) from it. The helm regains `1d3` expended charges daily at dawn.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 268. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
