# Bright Fungal Cloak
*Adventuring gear*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/FRHoF/Bright%20Fungal%20Cloak.webp#right)

- **Cost**: 25 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

*Never spelunk in the dark again! This cloak of bioluminescent fungus fibers is sure to brighten your day.*

While wearing a Bright Fungal Cloak, you can take a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) to furl or unfurl it. When the cloak is unfurled, it sheds [Bright Light](/rules/variant-rules/bright-light-xphb.md) in a 5-foot radius and [Dim Light](/rules/variant-rules/dim-light-xphb.md) for an additional 5 feet.

One pound of fungus is sewn into a Bright Fungal Cloak. This fungus can be eaten as food. Once all the fungus is consumed, the cloak becomes a mundane set of [Traveler's Clothes](/items/travelers-clothes-xphb.md).

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 132*
