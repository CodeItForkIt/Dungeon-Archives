# Pole of Collapsing
*Wondrous item, common*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/items/XDMG/Pole%20of%20Collapsing.webp#right)


This item functions as a Pole. While holding it, you can take a [Magic](/rules/actions.md#Magic) action to collapse it into a 1-foot-long rod for ease of storage (the pole's weight doesn't change) or cause the 1-foot-long rod to revert to a Pole. The rod elongates only as far as the surrounding space allows.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 286*
