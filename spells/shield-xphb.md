# Shield
*1st-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction, which you take when you are hit by an attack roll or targeted by the Magic Missile spell
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

An imperceptible barrier of magical force protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from [Magic Missile](/spells/magic-missile-xphb.md).
