# Searing Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action, which you take immediately after hitting a target with a Melee weapon or an [Unarmed Strike](/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md)
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 1 minute

As you hit the target, it takes an extra `1d6` Fire damage from the attack. At the start of each of its turns until the spell ends, the target takes `1d6` Fire damage and then makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the spell continues. On a successful save, the spell ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** All the damage increases by `1d6` for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Cleric (Forge Domain)](/lists/list-spells-classes-forge-domain-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB"); [Paladin](/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)
