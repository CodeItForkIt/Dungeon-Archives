# Greater Invisibility
*4th-level, Illusion*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

A creature you touch has the [Invisible](/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the spell ends.
