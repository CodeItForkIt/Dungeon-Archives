# Armor of Agathys
*1st-level, Abjuration*  
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/5etools-mirror-3/5etools-img/main/spells/XPHB/Armor%20of%20Agathys.webp#right)

- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S, M (a shard of blue glass)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

Protective magical frost surrounds you. You gain 5 [Temporary Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md). If a creature hits you with a melee attack roll before the spell ends, the creature takes 5 Cold damage. The spell ends early if you have no [Temporary Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The [Temporary Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md) and the Cold damage both increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 1.

**Classes**: [Paladin (Oath of Conquest)](/lists/list-spells-classes-oath-of-conquest-xge.md "subclass=XGE;class=XPHB"); [Warlock](/lists/list-spells-classes-warlock.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 243*
