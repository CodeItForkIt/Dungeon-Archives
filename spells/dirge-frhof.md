# Dirge
*6th-level, Enchantment*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (60-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 minute

Deathly power fills a 60-foot [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you for the duration.

When you cast this spell, you can designate creatures to be unaffected by it. Any other creature can't regain [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) while in the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Whenever the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) enters a creature's space and whenever a creature enters the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) or ends its turn there, the creature makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes `3d10` Necrotic damage and has the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage and its [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is halved. A creature makes this save only once per turn.

## Casting as a Circle Spell

Casting this as a Circle spell requires a minimum of two secondary casters. If the spell is cast as a Circle spell, its duration becomes [Concentration](/rules/conditions.md#Concentration), up to 10 minutes.

A creature that fails its save against the spell's effect also gains 1 [Exhaustion](/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level. While the creature has [Exhaustion](/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) levels, finishing a [Long Rest](/rules/variant-rules/long-rest-xphb.md) neither restores lost [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) nor reduces the creature's [Exhaustion](/rules/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

When the spell is cast, each secondary caster must expend a level 4+ spell slot; otherwise, the spell fails.
