# Cacophonic Shield
*3rd-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (10-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Thunderous reverberations fill a 10-foot [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) originating from you for the duration. Whenever the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) enters a creature's space and whenever a creature enters the [Emanation](/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) or ends its turn there, the creature makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the creature takes `3d6` Thunder damage and has the [Deafened](/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition until the start of your next turn. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage only. A creature makes this save only once per turn. When you cast this spell, you can designate creatures to be unaffected by it.

In addition, you have [Resistance](/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Thunder damage, and ranged attack rolls against you are made with [Disadvantage](/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md).

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d6` for each spell slot level above 3.
