# Elminster's Elusion
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

Arcane wards protect you against magic for the duration. You have [Advantage](/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws against spells and magical effects. Additionally, if you succeed on a saving throw against a spell or magical effect and would normally take half as much damage, you instead take no damage.
