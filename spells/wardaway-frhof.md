# Wardaway
*1st-level, Abjuration*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a miniature clay hand)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You hurl a disorienting magical force toward one creature within range. The target makes a Constitution saving throw; Constructs and Undead automatically succeed on this save.

On a failed save, the target takes `2d4` Force damage, its [Speed](/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is halved until the start of your next turn, and on its next turn, it can take only an action or a [Bonus Action](/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md) (but not both). On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage only.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `2d4` for every spell slot level above 1.
