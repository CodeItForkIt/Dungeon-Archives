# Laeral's Silver Lance
*3rd-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (120-foot Line)
- **Components:** V, S, M (a silver pin worth 250+ GP)
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Silver energy bursts out from you in a 120-foot-long, 5-foot-wide [Line](/rules/variant-rules/line-area-of-effect-xphb.md). Each creature of your choice in the [Line](/rules/variant-rules/line-area-of-effect-xphb.md) makes a Strength saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes `3d10` Force damage and has the [Prone](/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The damage increases by `1d10` for every spell slot level above 3.
