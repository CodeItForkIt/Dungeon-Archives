# Simbul's Synostodweomer
*7th-level, Transmutation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 hour

You imbue one creature you touch with magical healing energy for the duration. Whenever the target casts a spell using a spell slot, the target can immediately roll a number of unexpended [Hit Point Dice](/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md) equal to the spell slot's level and regain [Hit Points](/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll's total plus your spellcasting ability modifier; those dice are then expended.

**Classes**: [Bard](/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Arcana Domain)](/lists/list-spells-classes-arcana-domain-scag.md "subclass=SCAG;class=XPHB"); [Sorcerer](/lists/list-spells-classes-sorcerer.md); [Wizard](/lists/list-spells-classes-wizard.md)

*Source: Forgotten Realms: Heroes of Faerûn p. 145*
